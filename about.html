About Lux Travel

At Lux Travel, we specialize in curating unforgettable journeys that blend luxury, comfort, and authenticity. Founded by passionate explorers, our company was built on one simple idea — travel should feel effortless and extraordinary.

We plan every detail of your trip — from flight bookings and accommodations to hidden-gem experiences that only locals know about. Whether you dream of relaxing by the turquoise beaches of Bali, exploring the charm of Paris, or hiking in the Swiss Alps — we make it happen, flawlessly.

  • Tailor-made itineraries designed for each traveler
  • Exclusive partnerships with global resorts and airlines
  • 24/7 support anywhere in the world
  • Trusted by over 10,000 happy travelers
